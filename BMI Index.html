BMI Index

Body mass index is a value derived from the mass and height of a person.

BMI is defined a ass divided by the square of the body height, and is expressed in units of kg/m², resulting from mass in kilograms and height in metres.

BMI Category
16.0 and less Severely Underweight
16.0 - 18.4 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 - 34.9 Moderately Obese
35.0 - 39.9 Severely Obese
40.0 and more Morbidly Obese
